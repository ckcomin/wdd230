Scenarios

Scenario 1: How can I join the Chamber? The site will contain a join tab where the viewer can eaily find the steps and process to join.

Scenario 2: What are the local busniess in this area? The site will have a directory page where anyone can find a list of the local businesses in the area.

Scenario 3: What type of infrastructure should I be building in this area? The site will have a directory where you can see the businesses in the area and what they do. From here you can see if they are resturants, stores, or any other type of business that requires a type of building.

Scenario 4: What events are going on in my town? The site will have a discover page where people can see all of the upcoming events in the town.
