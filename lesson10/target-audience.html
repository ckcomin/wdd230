Target Audience

Temple workers, patrons, and their families

Personas

Temple Worker: Man named Bob who is temporarly working at the local tempele. He is in his mid thirties and a life-long memeber. He is married with one kid. Bob is fimilar with technology and can nagivate the web quite well.

Bride: Woman named Jen who is about to get married. She is in her mid twenties and a returned missonary. Her whole immediate family and her Finace's family are attending the temple wedding. Jen can navigate simple websites.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: How can I reserve a room? The site will contain a reservations tab where the viewer can eaily find the steps and process to reserve a room.

Scenario 2: Can I have a reception at this horel? The site will have a services page with weddings. Here it will talk about how to have a reception at the hotel

Scenario 3: Which temples are these hotels at? The site will have a temple directory where you can see the temple in the area. From here you can see if this hotel best suits your need for the temple it is by.

Scenario 4: Who can I contact for more information? The site will contact information on the home page to help with this. There will als be some contact info in the footer of each page.

Scenario 5: What amenities does this hotel come with? There will be a services page which will have all the amenities. Here the viewer can see all the things the hotels offer them.
